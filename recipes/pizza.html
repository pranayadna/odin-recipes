Pizza

Description

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

    • All-purpose flour
      Dry yeast
      Warm water
      Onion
      Tomatoes
      Capsicum
      Different kinds of cheese
      Tomato ketchup

    • Steps

    • Prepare the pizza dough
      Prepare the pizza base
      Chop all the vegetables for the pizza
      Spread the sauce and veggies on the base
      Bake the pizza at 250 degree Celsius for 10 minutes
