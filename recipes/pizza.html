Pizza

Description

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Dry yeast
  • Warm water
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Capsicum
  • Different kinds of cheese
  • Tomato ketchup

Steps

  1. Prepare the pizza dough
  2. Prepare the pizza base
  3. Chop all the vegetables for the pizza
  4. Spread the sauce and veggies on the base
  5. Bake the pizza at 250 degree Celsius for 10 minutes
